Instructions

Welcome to Fruity Fun, the game where you have to guess the value of the fruit shown.

Simply enter your guesses into the boxes and click submit. Feedback will be provided, and to try the next question, click the game button. If you need a challenge, select the Fruity game mode - good luck!
