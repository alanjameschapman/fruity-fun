Welcome to Fruity Fun, the game where you have to guess the value of the fruit shown below. Values range from 1 to 7 units.

Simply enter your guesses into the boxes and click submit. Feedback will be provided, and your score tally will update. To try the next question, click 'Next'. If you need a challenge, select Fruity - good luck!

Pssst... This maths game is actually GCSE-level simultaneous equations in disguise. For tips on how to solve these, click here.
